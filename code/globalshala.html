Screenshots and Descriptions

1. Comparing Amount Spent in INR and Reach

On compairing the Reach of each campaign with the total amount spent on each campaign, it can be analysed that campaigns 3, 4, 10, and 11 have less reach. However, the amount spent on these campaigns are almost similar to 6 and 7 which have better reach. So, campaign 3 and 11 are the least cost effective and have least reach.



2. Comparing Impressions and Reach

Upon analyzing the visualization, it can be seen that campaign 3 and 11 have the lease Reach and Impression. Also it can be seen that Reach and Impressions are correlated(Except Campaign 6) for each campaigns.

3. Comparing Reach in different regions

In this visualization, as the campaign 3 and 11 has the shorter circles than others which indicated that they have least reach in that targeted region.

4. Comparing Reach per Click with Impressions and Unique link click

In this visualization, it can be seen that campaign 3, 4, 10, and 11 have low reached and impressions. Additionally, 3 and 11 have extremely low Unique Link Clicks while 10 is close to none. Which indicates to discontinue them.

5. Comparing CPC, CPR and Reach Collectively

Analyzing the graph it can be seen that with the lowest reach campaign 3 and 10 have very low reached as compared to others and the Cost Per Click(CPC) and Cost Per Result(CPR) is very high which means that these are not cost effective.

6. How much will you save?
